About This Project

Mission

Our mission is to ensure safe drinking water availability and promote water facility management awareness across the campus community.

Vision

A campus where every student has access to safe drinking water and understands the importance of water conservation and hygiene.

Objectives

  • Survey all water points on campus
  • Identify and report water quality issues
  • Conduct awareness sessions
  • Promote water conservation practices
  • Create community engagement
